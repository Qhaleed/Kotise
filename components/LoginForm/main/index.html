FAQs

What does "JDM" stand for?

"JDM" stands for Japanese Domestic Market. It refers to vehicles, parts, and accessories that are designed and manufactured specifically for the Japanese market.

What makes a car a "JDM build"?

A JDM build typically refers to a vehicle that has been modified or customized using Japanese aftermarket parts, tuning techniques, and styling cues.

Are JDM cars street legal outside of Japan?

Whether a JDM car is street legal outside of Japan depends on local regulations and compliance with safety, emissions, and importation requirements.

How can I import a JDM car?

Importing a JDM car involves navigating various legal and logistical requirements, depending on your country's regulations.
